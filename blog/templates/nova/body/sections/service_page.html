About Us

The hackingEducation initiative exists to tackle barriers to education by addressing inequalities arising from intersections of race, class and gender through a community-centered teaching approach that encourages independent learning and promotes an 'each one, teach one' philosophy.

  • Projects based learning to supplement theoretical understanding from curriculum.
  • Building confidence, motivation and aspirations amongst students to pursue STEM.
  • Careers advice embedded within the session to demonstrate the unique opportunities open to STEM students.
