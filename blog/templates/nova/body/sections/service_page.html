About Us

We are a non-profit organisation comprised of approximately 20 volunteers from various backgrounds: academics, phD and undergraduates students, professionals, artists, youth workers and teachers. Our broad aim is to end barriers to education by addressing inequalities derived from intersections of race, class, and gender. We aim to do this through community centred teaching approaches that encourages independent learning, and is in line with our “each one, teach one” ideology.
