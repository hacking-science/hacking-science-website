Welcome to Hacking Education

We're an eduational charity that promotes access to education

Informal science

Learning sessions at schools, libraries and youth centres in Hackney where students are introduced to a new area of science and nudged towards independently researching the area as well as career opportunities in the field.

Coding

Programming sessions integrated with the science sessions where young people create their own electronics project using Raspberry Pis, MicroBits and Arduinos under the supervision of mentors.

Environment

Linking to the science sessions but dedicated session discussing environmental issues, solutions and the urgency with which we must act on those solutions.

Creative Spaces

Reimagining libraries as creative spaces where young people from the local community can collaborate on projects that explore ways to use science to make cool art.

History

Lecture series on the History of Race in the UK and Civil Rights. 100 sixth form students attend a 3-day event where we create a free online course on the history of race in the UK.

Magazine

Producing printed media aimed at young people and parents discussing topical scientific topics through articles, comic strips and illustrations.
